About

Hi! I am a third-year BSc student in Data Science and Technology at The Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST). I'm currently an exchange student at École Polytechnique Fédérale de Lausanne (EPFL) in the Computer Science section.

My research interests primarily focus on computer vision and machine learning. Specifically, I am particularly interested in developing fundamental methods to fully leverage large-scale world knowledge. My recent interests are particularly centered on 3D vision.

I have been fortunate to work with Prof. Xiaomeng Li on medical image analysis, and Prof. Chi-Keung Tang and Prof. Yu-Wing Tai on computer vision and multi-modal reasoning. I'm also fortunate to collaborate with Prof. Shangzhe Wu on 3D generation.

Research

My research focuses on computer vision and machine learning. My recent works explore:
