**Abstract**: We present ReasonNav, a novel framework that leverages Multimodal Large Language Models (MLLMs) for global reasoning and a deterministic planner for local navigation, creating a reason-then-act paradigm for embodied AI navigation.

**Key Contributions**:
- Developed a novel reason-then-act paradigm combining MLLMs with deterministic planning
- Enabled unified, zero-shot navigation across diverse tasks (object, image, text goals)
- Eliminated the need for task-specific fine-tuning or reinforcement learning
- Demonstrated superior performance in efficiency and reliability
